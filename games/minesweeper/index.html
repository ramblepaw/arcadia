How to Play Minesweeper

Goal

Reveal every cell on the board that isn't hiding a mine.

Controls

Click (or tap) a hidden cell to reveal it. Right-click a hidden cell to flag it as a suspected mine. Double-click a revealed number once you've flagged that many mines around it to auto-reveal its remaining neighbors. On touch devices, use the flag toggle in the header to switch taps between revealing and flagging.

Numbers

A revealed number tells you how many mines are hiding in the up-to-8 cells touching it. Use that to work out where it's safe to click next.

Winning & Losing

Reveal every safe cell to clear the field and win. Reveal a mine and the game ends immediately.
