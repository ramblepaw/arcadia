How to Play Pyramid

Goal

Clear every card out of the pyramid before you run out of cards in the stock.

Setup

28 cards are dealt into a triangular pyramid of 7 rows (1 card at the apex, 7 across the base). Only the base row starts face-up - every card above it is hidden until the two cards it rests on are both cleared. The remaining 24 cards form the stock, and the top card is flipped to become your starting guide card.

Your Turn

Click any face-up, uncovered pyramid card whose rank is exactly one higher or one lower than the guide card. That card is removed and becomes the new guide card. Aces count as both high and low, so a King or a 2 can follow an Ace.

Stuck?

If nothing in the pyramid matches, click the stock to draw a new guide card. You can also draw voluntarily at any time, even with a legal play available.

Winning & Losing

Clear all 28 pyramid cards to win. If the stock runs out while no pyramid card matches the guide card, the game ends - you lose with however many cards remain uncleared.
