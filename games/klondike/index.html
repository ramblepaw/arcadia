How to Play Klondike

Goal

Move all 52 cards onto the four foundations, sorted by suit from Ace up to King.

Setup

The tableau has 7 columns; column N (counting from 1) gets N cards, with only the top card of each column dealt face up. The other 24 cards form the stock.

Your Turn

Click a card to select it, then click where it should go - a highlighted pile or column marks a legal destination. Click the same card again to deselect it.

Tableau columns build downward in rank with alternating colors (red on black, black on red). Only a King may start an empty column. You can move a face-up card together with the valid run stacked on top of it.

Foundations build upward by suit, starting with the Ace.

Click the stock to draw one or three cards onto the waste pile (your choice, set before dealing). Only the top card of the waste is playable. Once the stock is empty, click it again to recycle the waste back into the stock - you can redeal as many times as you like.

Double-click a waste or tableau card to send it straight to its foundation, if that move is legal.

Winning

Get all 52 cards onto the foundations. There's no forced loss here - if you get stuck, just head back to the Main Menu and deal a new hand.
