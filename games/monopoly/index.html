Monopoly

How to Play Monopoly

Goal

Bankrupt every other player. Buy properties as you land on them, collect rent when others land on yours, and build houses and hotels to raise the stakes.

Your Turn

Roll two dice and move. Landing on an unowned property lets you buy it or send it to auction. Landing on a rival's property charges you rent - more if they own the whole color group, much more with houses or a hotel. Rolling doubles gives you another roll immediately; roll three doubles in a row and you go straight to Jail instead.

Building

Once you own every property in a color group, you can build houses (and eventually a hotel) on them from the Manage Properties screen - but you must build evenly across the group, one at a time.

Mortgaging & Trading

Short on cash? Mortgage a property (sell its houses first) for half its price. Want a property someone else owns? Propose a trade - cash, properties, or both.

Jail

While in Jail you can pay $50, use a Get Out of Jail Free card, or try to roll doubles. Fail three times and you're forced to pay and move.

Going Bankrupt

If you can't cover a debt even after mortgaging everything and selling all your houses, you're out. Last player standing wins.
