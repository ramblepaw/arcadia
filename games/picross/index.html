How to Play Picross Mosaic

Goal

One big picture is cut into a grid of small panels. Solve each panel as its own ordinary picross puzzle, and the full picture assembles as you clear panels.

Clues

Each panel has its own row and column clues, same as any picross puzzle: a clue like "3, 2" means a block of 3 filled cells, at least one gap, then a block of 2 filled cells, somewhere in that line.

Controls

Click a blank panel on the overview to open it. Inside a panel, click (or tap) a cell to fill it - click a filled cell again to clear it. Toggle mark mode (the ✕ button, or right-click on desktop) to mark cells you're confident are blank, as a memory aid; marks don't count toward solving.

Winning

A panel solves itself automatically the moment it matches its picture, and you're dropped back to the overview to pick the next one. Clear every panel to finish the mosaic.
