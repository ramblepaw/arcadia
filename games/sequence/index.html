Sequence

How to Play Sequence

Goal

Be the first to form the required number of Sequences - five of your chips in an unbroken line, horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. Corner spaces are free and count for everyone. Head-to-head games (2 players) need 2 sequences; three or four players need just 1.

Your Turn

Pick a card from your hand, then place a chip on a matching open space on the board - every card appears twice on the board, so pick either copy. Play a card, and you'll automatically draw a new one to refill your hand.

Jacks

Two-eyed jacks (♦ ♣) are wild - place a chip on any open space. One-eyed jacks (♥ ♠) let you remove one opponent chip from the board instead of placing your own - but chips that are already part of a completed sequence are locked and safe.

Dead Cards

If both board spaces for a card in your hand are already covered, that card is dead. Discard it to draw a replacement instead of placing a chip.
