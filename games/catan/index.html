How to Play Catan

Goal

Be the first to reach 10 victory points by settling the island, building cities, trading resources, and holding Longest Road or Largest Army.

Setup

In turn order (then reversed), each player places two settlements and two connecting roads. Your second settlement grants starting resources from every hex it touches.

Your Turn

Roll two dice. Every hex whose number matches the roll produces one resource per adjacent settlement (two for a city) - unless the robber sits on it. Rolling a 7 forces anyone with more than 7 cards to discard half, then you move the robber onto any hex and steal a card from someone touching it.

Building

Spend resources to build roads (wood + brick), settlements (wood + brick + sheep + wheat), or upgrade a settlement into a city (2 wheat + 3 ore). Settlements must be at least two roads away from any other settlement, and new roads must connect to your existing network.

Development Cards

Buy one for sheep + wheat + ore. Knights move the robber and count toward Largest Army (3+ played, most wins the bonus). Road Building gives two free roads, Year of Plenty gives any two resources, Monopoly takes every copy of one resource from all opponents. Victory Point cards add silently to your score.

Trading

Trade with the bank at 4:1, or better at a port your settlement/city touches (3:1 generic, 2:1 for a matching resource). You can also propose a trade to your opponents - the first to accept takes the deal.

Longest Road & Largest Army

Each is worth 2 points once you qualify (5+ continuous roads, or 3+ knights played) and stays with you until someone else strictly beats your total.

Discard Resources

You must discard cards (rolled a 7).
